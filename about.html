Company history at a glance

Established in 1996 under the mentorship of Mr.Silva Menon in Chennai. In 1997 Aster entered into a technical collaboration with Thomsun Consulting, a global healthcare consulting major, to provide technology driven consulting services specifically to the Indian Healthcare Sector. In 1998, Aster assisted the first Hospital in the country to get the Quality Certification, the Mallya Hospital at Bangalore. Subsequently over the next 10 years, Aster assisted over 100 Hospitals in the Private Sector to go in for technology Improvements leading to the State of the art facilities.
